About Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce the amount of waste that ends up in our oceans by selling aluminum water bottles. We donate all of our profits to The Ocean Cleanup because we believe in change. We especially want to be apart of that change. REVIVE strives to help stop plastic pollution that occurs in our waterways everyday. In order to clean the oceans, we needs to stop polluting the environment around us. How can we do that? ... By using 100% aluminum recycled waterbottles. Our waterbottles are BPA Free, made from 100% Recycled Materials and all of our profits go to the Ocean Cleanup!

How You Can Help

Do you want to be apart of the change? Do you want to help make a difference? You can help make a change my purchasing one of our environmentally friendly waterbottles. Our waterbottles are made of 100% recycled materials and BPA free. We aim to help reduce plastic pollution by creating reusable waterbottles. In fact, we donate all of our profits to The Ocean Cleanup because every purchase makes a difference. You can also help prevent plastic pollution by joining The Ocean Cleanup. You can learn more about The Ocean Cleanup, their mission and how to sign up in our Sign Up page. Help us make a change, one bottle at a time.
